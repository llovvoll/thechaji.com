We've been Making The Delicious Drink Since 2020

1. The choice of Tea

We use 100% Assam black tea and jasmine green tea imported from Taiwan, and pay great attention to the time of cooking tea has reduced the astringency and bitterness of the tea.

2. The choice of Bubble

Handmade brown sugar bubble imported from Taiwan is selected, slowly boiled for three hours and steamed, and finally soaked in syrup, That's why Chaji's Bubble is so delicious.

3. Persistence of quality

After receiving the order, we will make elaborate each drink, just want to bring customers unforgettable experience for drink.
